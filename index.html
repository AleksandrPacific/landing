Logistics

For a consumer living in a small village, it is difficult to deliver fresh herbs. It is difficult to ensure delivery, it can arrive already spoiled. In this case, the best option would be to install one small farm in the village.

Product quality

When growing vegetables or fruits using a soil surface, there is a risk of the plant acquiring any diseases or the spread of pathogenic flora, which will lead to the death of the crop. Automated farms are able to reduce scrap rates to a minimum. Smart farms use soilless plant growing methods.
